Educational credential assessment (ECA)

An Educational credential assessment (ECA) is used to verify that your foreign degree, diploma, or certificate (or other proof of your credential) is valid and equal to a Canadian one. There are different types of ECAs. Year by Year Evaluation (for Immigration Applicants), Course by course Evaluation (for Student Visa Applicants) and Agri-Food Pilot ECA. Without an ECA report your application will not be accepted by the Citizenship and Immigration Canada (CIC).

You must include your ECA report and the reference number in your Express Entry or PNP application profile.
