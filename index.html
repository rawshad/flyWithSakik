About Us

FLY WITH SAKIK (FWS) is a professional Immigration consulting firm. We are committed to provide true and complete information to our honorable potential clients. Our expert, dedicated and professional service will help you to touch your dream with low cost. FWS also provides a complete Educational Credential Assessment (ECA) verification service from all universities and medical colleges. We are able to do ECA verification from WES, CES, IQAS, ICES, MCC and PEBC. Our ECA One Stop Service will give you a new test of ECA verification easily and get your report from recognized authority, without hassle. Just authorize us to do your ECA, our expert team will take complete responsibility to get it done. We are also providing work permit visa service for low skill workers, study abroad, visit visa service and IELTS Course (full and crash course).
